# 🦅 Striving Eagle - Netlify Customization Guide

## Change Your Netlify URL

### Option 1: Rename Site (Free)
1. Go to your Netlify dashboard
2. Click on your site: "lovely-heliotrope-94d7ae"
3. Click **"Site settings"**
4. Under **"Site information"** → **"Change site name"**
5. Enter: `striving-eagle` 
6. Click **"Save"**
7. New URL: `striving-eagle.netlify.app`

### Option 2: Custom Domain (Free with Netlify)
1. In Site settings → **"Domain management"**
2. Click **"Add custom domain"**
3. Enter: `strivingeagle.netlify.app` (free subdomain)
4. Or buy `strivingeagle.com` through Netlify

### Option 3: SEO Title & Description
Add this to your `index.html` head section:

```html
<!-- SEO Meta Tags -->
<title>Striving Eagle - Premium Fashion Store</title>
<meta name="description" content="Striving Eagle - Your premium destination for fashion and style. Shop our latest collection of clothing and accessories.">
<meta property="og:title" content="Striving Eagle - Fashion Store">
<meta property="og:description" content="Premium fashion store with latest trends">
<meta property="og:url" content="https://striving-eagle.netlify.app">
```

### Option 4: Branded Search Results
1. Google will show: **"Striving Eagle"** once you change the site name
2. Add the SEO meta tags above
3. Submit to Google Search Console

## Quick Steps:
1. Change site name to `striving-eagle`
2. Update SEO meta tags
3. Wait for Google to index (1-3 days)

Your search results will show "Striving Eagle" instead of the random name!
